### Updates on Frosty

After working on the adding a requested feature last week, I pivoted to a unique feature that hasn't been mentioned before. Currently, you can pin multiple chatrooms to your dashboard and swipe through to see what's happening. Other third-party Twitch apps have a **mentions only** chat that only shows messages where you've been @mentioned. These projects are also open-source and I've looked into their codebase and how that can be integrated into Frosty.

### Personal contributions 

I regularly use [watchparty.me](https://www.watchparty.me/) and worked on a feature that allows users to popout the chatbox while they're sharing their screen. I created the [PR](https://github.com/howardchung/watchparty/pull/1053) and recieved feedback from the maintainer.

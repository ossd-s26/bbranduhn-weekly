### OSS in Industry

It was interesting to see the extensive list of open-source software being in the film industry. I didn't raise concerns during lecture, but it raised a concern I have: that major film studios have become less motivated to innovate when existing pipelines can easily achieve certain goals. 

Films that are rightfully praised for pushing boundaries in animation, VFX, and cinemetography tend to do so by inventing new techniques and approaches, rather than relying on established tools.

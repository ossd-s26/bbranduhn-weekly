### Progress

During the break, I looked through the codebase and tried to understand what's possible in terms of new features and debugging. Two issues interested me 
- Visual identifiers corresponding to individual users mismatched amongst the chatroom
- Mention/Block users doesn't exists 

One issue I encountered while solving the mismatched visual indentifiers bug was that it's hard to replicate. Twitch added a [shared chat](https://help.twitch.tv/s/article/shared-chat) feature that lets two chats jointly exist. Not many streamers use the feature so when I'm attempting to debug the issue, I have to find a community using the feature and run tests before they decide to end sharing chats. I've put that issue on the shelf and pivoted to my second interest.

In tight knit communities, even massively expanding ones, users become familiar with themselves and care about what other users specifically said. Twitch themselves added a feature to individually highlight messages from users. Because of how recent this was, Frosty doesn't have this feature. I'm currently working on adding that feature in a seamless manner.
